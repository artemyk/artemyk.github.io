Intro

Google Scholar   |   CV   |   GitHub

I am currently working as a researcher at the Universal Biology Institute, a theoretical biology group at the University of Tokyo. Prior to this, I spent several years as a postdoc at the Santa Fe Institute, an interdisciplinary research institute dedicated to the study of complex systems.

In my research, I use techniques from information theory and statistical physics to understand information processing in various physical and biological complex systems. For more details, see my research page.

For a complete list of publications and preprints, see my Google Scholar page or download my CV.
